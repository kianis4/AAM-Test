## PART 5: OPERATIONS & COMPLIANCE (Guides 15-20) — NEW MATERIAL

### Process: Transfer (Assumption) → [Guide 15](15-Transfer-Process.md)

**When it applies:** Change of lessee, equipment change, or payment schedule change

- [ ] **Know the 10 required documents** → Appendix (A/G/assignment), PAD (Client B), E-Sign summary, ID (both clients), Insurance (unless GAI), Void cheque (Client B), Credit decision with new IFL#, 1st page original contract, IPC, REQ/Corp profile
- [ ] **Know the 3-team relay** → Sales (docs + POA) → Sales Support (create assumption in IFL, match payment grid) → Validation (validate, amend PPSA, Welcome Package)
- [ ] **Know the GAI exception** → If covered by GAI, skip insurance docs AND don't CC assurance@mhccna.com
- [ ] **Know the IFL fee validation steps** → Contract section → Charges → PG/CG → Contract Maintenance → Third Parties → Related Party Code
- [ ] **Know where things go** → Welcome Package saved in Vision under ORIGINAL file application, NOT the new one

### Process: Who Can Sign a Contract → [Guide 16](16-Authorized-Signatories.md)

**When it applies:** EVERY deal — someone has to sign the contract

- [ ] **Inc. (most common)** → Directors at REQ/Securfact sign. **BUT** check for unanimous shareholder agreement first (can transfer authority to shareholders). REQ shows it; Securfact does NOT.
- [ ] **Sole Proprietorship** → Owner only. Must have REQ/Securfact registration even though not legally required.
- [ ] **General Partnership (QC only)** → ALL partners sign + valid ID
- [ ] **Limited Partnership (L.P.)** → Only the commandités (general partners) sign. Commanditaires (limited partners) CANNOT sign — ever.
- [ ] **Cooperative** → Get 3 documents (resolution, Certificate of Conformity, internal regulations) and have Legal validate
- [ ] **Municipality** → Admin at REQ OR signatory from council meeting resolution
- [ ] **NPO** → Admin at REQ OR signatory from resolution excerpt. No registration obligation.
- [ ] **Partnership (ROC)** → General = ALL sign; Limited = General Partners only
- [ ] **Power of Attorney exception** → Third party can sign with POA approved by Credit, Legal, or CVC manager
- [ ] **Responsibility chain** → Credit (1st) → Person documenting (2nd) → Validation at V1/V2 if external (3rd)

### Process: Bill 96 — Quebec French Language → [Guide 17](17-Bill-96-Quebec-Language-Law.md)

**When it applies:** ANY deal touching Quebec (only 1 trigger needed)

- [ ] **4 triggers (any 1 is enough):** Client based in QC, signatory lives in QC, equipment used/plated in QC, guarantor lives in QC
- [ ] **Exception:** Does NOT apply to QC dealer signing SSC/buyback
- [ ] **For English contracts in QC, you need 3 things:**
  1. Proof client received FR version first AND explicitly requested EN (email chain, verifiable attachments)
  2. Equipment description in French on the contract (brands/models can stay in original language)
  3. RDPRM filed entirely in French (MHCCA name, client name, equipment, general collateral)
- [ ] **Acronym corrections you can do yourself:** VIN→NIV, SUV→VUS (per Clause 3)
- [ ] **Invoice exception:** If contract refs an invoice, get French invoice OR manually prepare Appendix A in French
- [ ] **Consequences:** RDPRM refusal (unsecured in 7 days!), contract/registration inconsistency, court invalidation, OQLF fines up to $30,000
- [ ] **No case law exists yet** → compliance is critical because outcomes are unpredictable

### Process: GAI Insurance → [Guide 18](18-GAI-Insurance-Program.md)

**When it applies:** Qualifying new contracts — fund without client COI

- [ ] **4 requirements (ALL must be met):** Not assignment, <$1MM, 12-84 months term, 100% covered assets (non-plated, no excluded categories)
- [ ] **Excavator exception:** ARE covered by GAI but Vision shows false warning. Fix: QC = Insurance tab (check box), Other provinces = Equipment tab (uncheck plated box)
- [ ] **If client sends COI on GAI deal:** Forward to 5860.VerifyInsurance@gaig.com. Do NOT validate. Do NOT enter in Vision. Post-funding: also CC insuranceca@mhccna.com
- [ ] **60-day timeline:** Client has 60 days to provide own insurance → then GAI force-places and bills client → reimbursement only from date of client's own coverage start
- [ ] **GAI handles 3 things:** Insurance follow-up, renewal during term, claims

### Process: Op/Cap Lease Restructure → [Guide 19](19-Op-Cap-Lease-Restructure.md)

**When it applies:** Client refinances residual value at end of OP or CAP lease

- [ ] **Why restructure instead of new deal?** → Maintains original PPSA registration (no security gap)
- [ ] **Forms:** CON15.7 (OP lease) or CON15.71 (CAP lease). PG signature must be on it if original deal had PG.
- [ ] **Buyout rules:** Full buyout, remove EOT fees, standard lease admin fee, stay within same entity (MHCCL→MHCCL)
- [ ] **PPSA:** Keep original → Amend reference number → Renew to match new term → Screenshot BEFORE saving → Validation submits
- [ ] **Vision Summary tab:** Vendor = MHCCL or MHCCA (use master record #152498 for MHCCA). DO NOT USE MHCCA ABL.
- [ ] **Vision Structure:** Equipment cost field = amount financed (NOT original cost). If MHCCA, choose "Refinancing Option" in DBA Name.
- [ ] **Vision Invoice tab:** Vendor = MHCCL or MHCCA. DO NOT USE LESSOR (won't balance). Invoice # = buyout number. Tax Province = "No tax."

---

### Process: Driver's License Validation → [Guide 20](20-Drivers-License-Validation.md)

**When it applies:** Every deal requiring ID verification (both Sales and Operations validate)

- [ ] **Provinces that encode name + DOB in license number:** Ontario, Quebec, Nova Scotia, Newfoundland (Format 1), Manitoba (name only)
- [ ] **Ontario trick:** Last 6 digits = YYMMDD, females add 50 to month. First letter = last name.
- [ ] **Quebec trick:** A####DDMMYY##. Expiry = birthday. Gold-to-green ink at fleur-de-lys. All fields in French.
- [ ] **Manitoba trick:** First 5 = last name, 6th = first name initial, 7th = middle initial. * if missing.
- [ ] **Nova Scotia:** LLLLLDDMMYYXXX (5 letters of last name + DOB + serial)
- [ ] **Alberta:** Most security features of any province — 3 transparent windows, Albertosaurus, rainbow printing
- [ ] **7 universal checks (all provinces):** Raised lettering, holographic elements, ghost photo, tactile surface, visual inspection, card quality, barcode alignment

## PART 6: CROSS-GUIDE CONNECTIONS

These are situations where multiple guides intersect. The test LOVES these.

### Situation: Quebec Construction Deal
- **Credit Box** (Guide 14): Construction CR and tier rules apply
- **Bill 96** (Guide 17): Equipment in QC = RDPRM in French, French-first contract process
- **Authorized Signatories** (Guide 16): Check who can sign based on company type
- **Driver's License** (Guide 20): If QC license, verify A####DDMMYY## format

### Situation: Transferring a GAI-Covered Contract
- **Transfer Process** (Guide 15): Full transfer procedure
- **GAI** (Guide 18): Skip insurance docs, don't CC assurance@mhccna.com
- **Authorized Signatories** (Guide 16): Client B must be authorized signatory for their company type

### Situation: Operating Lease Ending — Client Wants to Keep Equipment
- **Operating Lease Training** (Guide 06): Understand OP lease vs CAP lease
- **Lease Restructure** (Guide 19): CON15.7, maintain PPSA, Vision integration
- **Taxation** (Guide 11): Tax implications of switching from OP to CAP

### Situation: Atlantic Canada Deal with AITC
- **AITC** (Guide 07): 10% credit, 7 industries, 5 regions, 8-step process
- **GAI** (Guide 18): If deal qualifies for GAI, no insurance needed before funding
- **Bill 96** (Guide 17): If equipment in Gaspe Peninsula (Quebec), Bill 96 applies!
- **Credit Box** (Guide 14): Normal RAC criteria still apply on top of AITC

### Situation: Forestry Deal in Quebec with English-Speaking Client
- **Credit Box** (Guide 14): $0 auto-decision, max 60mo, NO analyst exceptions
- **Bill 96** (Guide 17): French-first process required, RDPRM in French
- **AITC** (Guide 07): If new equipment + Atlantic region (Gaspe or NB/NS/PEI/NL), AITC may apply
- **Driver's License** (Guide 20): Validate QC license format

### Situation: Client Company Changes Ownership Mid-Lease
- **Transfer Process** (Guide 15): Change of lessee = full transfer procedure
- **Authorized Signatories** (Guide 16): New owners must be authorized for the company type
- **GAI** (Guide 18): Check if original contract was GAI-covered (affects insurance requirements)
